About Me

My journey into video editing started with a fascination for short-form content and how visual rhythm shapes attention and emotion. What began as simple experimentation gradually evolved into a more structured approach focused on pacing, cuts, and visual storytelling.

With After Effects, I mainly create clean and minimal text animations integrated directly into footage, focusing on timing, readability, and visual impact. Alongside this, I use DaVinci Resolve as my main tool for editing, sequencing, and color grading.

My workflow is centered around clarity, rhythm, and intention rather than complex motion graphics. I prefer controlled animations that enhance the footage instead of distracting from it.

My goal is to continuously improve with every project, strengthen my technical foundation, and progressively expand toward more advanced motion design as my skills evolve.
